About Us
Lolaniyi Ventures was established in 2003, and has been operating functioning till date in Lekki-Epe Expressway axis.
Lolaniyi Ventures specializes in Haulage, Leasing, Hiring of earth moving equipment and Road/Building Construcions.
Mission
Our mission is to meet customer's increasing demands for our service. We will offer all of our customers the direct personal attention they deserve.Our commitment to customer service and satisfaction assures us that our customers are 100% satisfied with our services.
Vision
Our vision is to be the leading provider of reliable and innovative construction equipment rentals that empower our customers to achieve their goals with confidence.We aim to be a trusted partner for construction professionals, enabling them to build better and faster while minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity.

Our Services

Comprehensive
We offer a wide range of construction equipment rentals to meet the needs of any project, big or small.

We offer flexible hiring options to accommodate your project timeline and budget, including daily, weekly, and monthly rentals.
Expertise
Our team of experienced professionals can provide expert advice on equipment selection, operation, and maintenance to help you get the most out of your rental.
Safety

Support
We are committed to providing exceptional customer support throughout the rental process, from equipment selection to delivery and pickup.
